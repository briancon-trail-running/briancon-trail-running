Bivacco Berrone

Distance: 29.2KM | Elevation Gain: 2410M+

Dates accessible: June to September

Start co-ordinates: 45.071614, 6.878595

Route description

A brutal climb, a few summits above 3000m, and then a brutal descent. A few technical passages with cables. To make the downhill a little sweeter you will be rewarded with amazing views on the Ecrins massif.

If you want to break the route up into two days you can stay in the Bivacco, though during summer you'll want to arrive early (before 2pm) to get a spot.

Access

There is a small car park just outside of the village of Salbertrand.
