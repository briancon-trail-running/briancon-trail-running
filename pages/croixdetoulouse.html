La Croix de Toulouse

Distance: 6.1KM | Elevation Gain: 540M+

Season: All year!

Start co-ordinates: 44.90384139006228, 6.64655339868968

Route description

This is without doubt the trail I do the most often in Briançon. The chances are, if you decide to do it, you will probably come across me at some point.

Accessible all year round because of its southern exposition and relatively low altitude, La Croix de Toulouse has a little bit of everything -- some runnable sections, some technical sections, and great views all along. Though there are many options for getting up La Croix de Toulouse, my favourite is to go up on the west side and descend the steeper east side (Bois de la Pinée).

Watch out for mountain bikers in the summer!

Access

Directly accessible from Briançon.
