Col d'Arsine

Distance: 27.9KM | Elevation Gain: 1000M+

Dates accessible: June to September

Start co-ordinates: 45.071614, 6.878595

Route description

A steady climb through the woods takes you up below the glacier and the Lac d'Arsine. All of the ascent happens in the first 12km and from then on the route is a nice long downhill.

Before exiting out towards the road you will pass by a technical section with a cable that makes this route dangerous when snowy. Luckily this section is south facing so it's usually clear of snow early.

Access

There is a small car park just outside of the village of Le Lauzet.
